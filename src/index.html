ASI provides unique business intelligence for car dealerships in the US who are looking to improve their inventory turnover.

Big Data made simple

ASI scans millions of vehicle listings and uses advanced algorithms to analyze their sales patterns. Monitoring the time ads are staying online and tracking price alterations it estimates average time on market and sales prices for any configuration.

Dealers can easily identify fast selling configurations for any vehicle segment to cut sales cycles and maximize rate of return.

  • Defining segmentations are simply done using filters like make, model, mileage, features, location and many more.

  • Using segment definitions our system estimates how long it takes to sell a vehicle with the given configuration.

  • Side-by-side comparison of search results makes it easy to discover high demand configurations in no time.
